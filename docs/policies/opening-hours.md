# Opening Hours

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Northstar Outfitters customer support is available Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm UK time.

Messages received outside support hours are handled on the next working day.

## Response Times

Most support emails receive a first response within 1 business day.

During major sales, product launches, bank holidays, or severe weather disruptions, replies may take up to 2 business days.

Support does not provide live phone support.
Customers should contact support by email and include their order number when the question relates to an order.

## Weekend And Holiday Coverage

The support team does not usually answer messages on weekends or UK public holidays.

Urgent operational updates, such as major shipping disruption notices, may still be sent by email when needed.

## Time Zones

All stated support hours use UK time.

Customers in other countries may receive replies outside their local working hours because the support team operates from the United Kingdom.

## Contact

Customers with general questions should email support with a clear subject line and enough detail for the team to help.
